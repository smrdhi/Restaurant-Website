Welcome to SILVER SPOONS

Founded in 1999, Silver Spoons began as a small family-run eatery with a vision to bring people together over delicious, heartfelt meals.
What started as a humble passion project has grown into a beloved dining destination in Mumbai.
Rooted in tradition yet inspired by innovation, our menu reflects a perfect balance of classic recipes and modern culinary trends.


From our early days of serving homemade dishes to a handful of guests, we have always prioritized quality, freshness, and community.
Today, Silver Spoons continues to uphold its legacy of excellence, offering a cozy ambiance, exceptional service, and flavors that tell a story.
Join us and be a part of our journey, one spoonful at a time!
